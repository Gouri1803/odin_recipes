Turkey Burger recipe

home

Description

This turkey burger recipe is the best.

These are full of flavor — any cooking method may be used, and they freeze very well. The recipe can also be used for meatballs or meatloaves.

Ingredients

  1. 3 pounds ground turkey
  2. ¼ cup seasoned bread crumbs
  3. ¼ cup finely diced onion
  4. 2 egg whites, lightly beaten
  5. ¼ cup chopped fresh parsley
  6. 1 clove garlic, peeled and minced
  7. 1 teaspoon salt
  8. ¼ teaspoon ground black pepper

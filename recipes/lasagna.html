Description

This chicken lasagna is made with tender chicken breasts, red sauce, and lots of mozzarella cheese for a fantastic flavor combination. Everyone who tastes it raves!

This easy lasagna features jarred spaghetti sauce for easy layering and rich tomato flavor.

Ingredients

  1. 6 uncooked lasagna noodles
  2. 3 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves
  3. 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese, divided
  4. 1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
  5. ¼ cup hot water
  6. 1 cube chicken bouillon
  7. 1 (26 ounce) jar spaghetti sauce
